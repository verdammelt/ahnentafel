Ahnentafel is a simple program to display the contents of a family tree. The Family Tree data is read in from a file in GEDCOM format.

The intention is to use this application as a way of sharing a family tree (mine in this case) with far-flung relatives and then they can email me with additions and edits.

Currently it is a very simple, and not very pretty, application. It will become over time a nicer looking and more full featured application.
